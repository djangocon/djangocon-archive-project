The Conference Hotel

The Hilton Portland and Executive Tower

921 SW SIXTH AVENUE, PORTLAND, OREGON, 97204, USA
TEL: 1-503-226-1611 FAX: 1-503-220-2565

Register now - conference rates are only available for a limited time

The Hilton will be fondly remembered by those who attended DjangoCon US 2011, which we hosted at the same venue.

At the heart of the downtown district, with airport connections passing within a quarter of a mile, close to Portland's Pearl district and within a block of Pioneer Courthouse Square there is everything you will need to engage in a full week's enjoyment within walking distance of the conference venue.

We have negotiated what we feel is a remarkable rate for such quality accommodations, and we hope you will take advantage of our full date range to spend some time in Portland before or after the conference (or both!).

This year the daily room rate for single or double occupancy (we know some of you prefer to share to keep costs down) is $125. Some roooms can be booked for up to four occupants at slightly higher rates.

Register now - we look forward to seeing you in Portland for the social events before the conference!
