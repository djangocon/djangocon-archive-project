Patrick Altman

Patrick Altman is the VP of Engineering at Eldarion where he manages operations and engineering. In addition, he is a core developer on the Pinax project.

He helps to maintain over 100 open source projects many of which he is the primary author. Most of these projects are Django reusable apps that are part of the Pinax ecosystem and support Eldarion's vision of taking Ideas to Launch, Faster.
