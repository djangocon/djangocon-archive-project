About Dennis Bunskoek

Dennis Bunskoek is co-founder of Leukeleu B.V. and Ride The Pony B.V. in The Netherlands. Leukeleu and Ride The Pony consists of a team of 8 developers, who build Django-powered web sites and web apps, and work on Django Fiber - a simple, user-friendly CMS for all your Django projects.

Organisation/company Leukeleu B.V.

About Manuel Saelices

Manuel Saelices is CTO at Yaco Sistemas, a 30+ people company, mostly Django
developers. His experience in Python began in 2003 with the Plone CMS, and
in 2006 He fell in love with Django. He currently collaborates in some
Django applications as django-player, cmsutils, django-inlinetrans and
django-transmeta, and is the project leader and core developer of Merengue.

Organisation/company Yaco Sistemas
Website http://www.yaco.es/
Twitter @linator

About Jonas Obrist

I'm a passionate Python developer, and after a few years of
frameworkless Python CGI web development, I finally switched to Django
and pretty much instantly fell in love with it. Nowadays I am the lead
developer for the django CMS, one of the biggest reusable Django apps,
and have a number of other Django apps I maintain, most notably
django-classy-tags, django-sekizai and project-nani. During the day I
build websites using the django CMS for the Swiss web agency Divio, the
driving power behind the django CMS and the Django Shop projects.

Organisation/company Divio GmbH
