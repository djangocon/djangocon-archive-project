Sponsoring DjangoCon US

We are grateful for your interest in DjangoCon US, and will try to work with you to put together a package that best suits your needs. Sponsorships start at $1,000 and include placement on event signage, the conference program, and this web site. You may upgrade to include booth space, banner placement, tote bag inserts, etc.

We can work with you to put together a flexible package to include separate sponsorship of evening events, receptions, refreshments, wi-fi, etc. We're prepared to be creative to ensure your requirements are met.

Sponsorship Summary

For complete details, please download the sponsorship prospectus here or call 1-800-494-3119.

Financial Assistance for Delegates

This year, due to the high costs associated with the venue, The Open Bastion is contributing $10,000 for delegate scholarships to be awarded by the Django Software Foundation. If you would like to contribute to this fund, we'll include a special "thank you" on this web site (and the recipients a great deal of gratitude, as well). Please call 1-800-494-3119 or email info@theopenbastion.com to discuss.
