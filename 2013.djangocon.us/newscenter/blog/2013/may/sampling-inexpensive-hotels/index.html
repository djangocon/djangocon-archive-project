A Sampling of Inexpensive Hotels

May 23, 2013 5:16 p.m. —

Chicago, it turns out, has a number of relatively inexpensive hotels within walking distance of the Hyatt Regency.  Some of these are listed here  - the rates during the week of DjangoCon were determined on May 23, 2013, and are exclusive of taxes.

Wicker Park Inn  $139  http://www.wickerparkinn.com/

Old Chicago Inn  $ 35    http://www.centralloophotel.com/

Central Loop Hotel  $131  http://www.centralloophotel.com/

Villa Toscana Guest House  $119  http://www.villa-toscana.com/

Two Urns  $93  http://twourns.com/

Parthenon Guest House  $80  http://www.chicagoparthenonhostel.com/reservations/
