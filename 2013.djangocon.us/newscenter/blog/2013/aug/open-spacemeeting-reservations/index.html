Open Space/Meeting Reservations

Do you need space for a meeting or an Open Space session? You can reserve a room before the conference starts.

August 19, 2013 2:19 p.m. —

Do you need space for a meeting or an Open Space session? You can reserve a room before the conference starts.

Simply visit this Google form and fill out your requirements - the date/time input is free format, so you can ask for a number of alternative times quite easily. Please make sure your email address is correct, or we won't be able to confirm your request!

You will receive an email reply to confirm you have been scheduled.

At the conference we will put the schedule up on the whiteboard, and you will be able to make low-tech additions with marker pens on-site.

We hope you find this a useful facility. Brickbats and bouquets via the Contact Us form, please.
