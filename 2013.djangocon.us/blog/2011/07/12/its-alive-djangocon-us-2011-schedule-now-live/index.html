It's Alive! The Djangocon US 2011 Schedule is now live

12th July 2011 by Sean O'Connor

After receiving over 80 amazing proposals the review process is now complete and we present the schedule of talks for Djangocon US 2011!

We were blown away by all of the great proposals submitted to this year's conference. Choosing among them was incredibly difficult (we wanted to see all of them!) but we made it through and we are really happy with this year's program. Thank you to everybody who submitted a talk proposal and an extra big thanks to everybody on the organizers list who were key in putting together the schedule.

In addition to all of the great talks from the members of the community, we've managed to pull some strings and invite a number of really interesting keynote speakers. We hope they will help bring some new insights and perspectives to the Django community. These keynote speakers include David Eaves, Brad Fitzpatrick, and Glyph Lefkowitz.

Keep an eye on this blog as we will be posting more information about this year's keynotes as well as some of the most interesting talks over the next few weeks.
