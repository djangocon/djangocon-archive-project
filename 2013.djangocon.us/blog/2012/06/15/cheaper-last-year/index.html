Cheaper Than Last Year?

15th June 2012 by holdenweb

Not generally, we are afraid, but if you get in early enough you can go to DjangoCon this year for less than you did last year!

That's right. Thanks to the new Supersaver tickets, you can actually save money over last year's DjangoCon. The hotel is priced at the same rate as last year, but conference fees are up slightly. If you don't need to see the schedule before you decide whether to go these tickets are an excellent choice.

Student: $250; Individual: $375; Corporate $475. These tickets are on-sale now in very limited number, and only until the end of June: get them while they are available!
