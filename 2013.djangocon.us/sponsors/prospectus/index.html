Sponsoring DjangoCon US

Thank You!

We are grateful for your interest in DjangoCon US. Without your support this conference, the largest meeting of Django technologists in the world, would not be possible.

If you don't have time right now for the detailed prospectus, we have collected the most important advance information on this page.

We do have a number of standard packages, described in the sponsor prospectus. Our aim is always to help sponsors meet their goals for the conference, so we will be happy to entertain proposals that don't necessarily fit the standard packages.
