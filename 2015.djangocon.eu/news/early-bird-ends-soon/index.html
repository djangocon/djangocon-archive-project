Early-bird registration must end soon

This week, early-bird registration will end and standard ticket prices will apply. Take advantage of the lower prices while you still can.

We’re going to keep early-bird sales open as long as we dare, but our tight budget means we can’t afford to be careless.

At the current rate of sales, we have to end them before the end of this week. The standard ticket prices are £50 higher.

For anyone who hasn’t bought a ticket yet because they’re hoping that they’ll qualify for a grant or discounted ticket courtesy of the Django Software Foundation, please don’t wait if you can afford to buy a ticket now.

If you do subsequently obtain a grant or discounted ticket, you get a refund on the ticket you buy now, so you won’t have lost out. And even if you don’t, at least you’ll have a cheaper early-bird ticket.
