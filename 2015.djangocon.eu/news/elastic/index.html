Elastic, our Silver sponsor

Helping people make sense of data

Elastic is a company that believes getting insight from data matters.

Built around three open source products - Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana - Elastic is extending what’s possible with data, delivering on the promise that good things come from connecting the dots.

Designed to help users take data from any source and search, analyse, and visualise it in real time, Elastic products are changing the way organisations get value from data.
