Free crèche at DjangoCon

For the first time, DjangoCon will have a crèche for children. We hope that this will make it easier for attendees with young children to come to the event.

If you’ve ever wanted to go to a DjangoCon but weren’t able to because you have young children - maybe you can this time, and bring them with you.

The crèche will be staffed by registered and qualified childminders during conference hours on all six days of the event.

You will need to register well in advance for the crèche, or at the very least let us know what your requirements will be. We may simply be unable to obtain the services you need if you leave it too late.

Children will be welcome at all appropriate conference events including meals, during breaks for refreshments, in the audience at talks and where possible at social events.

As far as it is possible we want to make this a child-and-parent-friendly conference.

We hope that this helps some people attend who wouldn’t otherwise be able to, and that this will also help improve diversity.

25th January 2015
