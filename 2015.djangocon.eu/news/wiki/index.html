Our handy conference wiki

Get organised with the conference wiki: share a taxi, find a room-mate, arrange a meal.

Our wiki for attendees.

If you’d like to find a travel companion or a room-mate, list yourselves there. You’re likely to find other people who are willing to share, to save money or to make a journey easier.

For example, if you’re landing at Bristol airport, sharing a taxi to Cardiff with a few other people might be both cheaper and faster than taking the bus and train.

Or for example if you’re a Cardiff-based attendee with a room to let for the week, mention it on the wiki.

It’s your wiki, use it to make your trip better.
