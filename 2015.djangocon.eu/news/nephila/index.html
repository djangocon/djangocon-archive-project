Nephila, our Gold sponsor

Supportive friends of the Python/Django community since 2008, Nephila are enthusiastic participants in its projects and events.

Open-source software is part of Nephila’s DNA: they use it, create it and are committed to supporting it.

Nephila have been involved in the organisation of a number of Python/Django events, including Django Village, established in 2014 and PyCon Italia.

Clients of the company include organisations in the arts and cultural sectors, where their ability to integrate technology in contexts with very particular requirements and to understand needs that are not only expressed in specifications has been key to their success.

In the UK they work with clients in the manufacturing and technology sectors, typically with companies who have a multinational presence and a branch in the UK.

Nephila are heavily involved in the development of django CMS, contributing significantly to the project, its ecosystem and its community.

At Nephila they enjoy fine software, the best European beers and the most beautiful Italian cities, and sharing them all.
