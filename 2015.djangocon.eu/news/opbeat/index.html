Opbeat, our Gold sponsor

Helping developers to do great ops

Opbeat is the ops platform built for Django that combines metrics, release tracking, error logging and mobile alerts. It extends GitHub, is completely free for small teams, and sets up in minutes.

The guys and girls from Opbeat are working hard to simplify the post-shipping workflow, and are active members of Django Copenhagen. They consume substantial amounts of coffee in the process.

Opbeat will be sponsoring the coffee at this year’s DjangoCon, and will be recognisable by their coffee-pot t-shirts.

If you see a member of the Opbeat crew please feel free to give them a refill.
