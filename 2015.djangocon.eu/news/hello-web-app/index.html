Hello Web App at DjangoCon

Get a copy of Tracy Osborn’s brand new book at a special price, delivered directly to you at DjangoCon.

We’re very pleased that Tracy Osborn is bringing copies of Hello Web App to DjangoCon (launched just today, May the Fourth be with her etc).

Hello Web App is aimed squarely at beginners. Like the workshop, it’s tailored for designers rather than programmers, and especially recommended if you’re attending Tracy’s Django for web designers workshop.

The book takes you from basic HTML templates to a web launch. If you have an idea for a web-based product but no programming experience, this is just what you need.

For DjangoCon attendees the price is only US$19.95 (£13.50), and Tracy’s also making a contribution to our diversity fund with each copy sold.
