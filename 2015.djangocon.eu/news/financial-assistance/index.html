Financial assistance & free tickets - apply now

The Django Software Foundation has announced US$5000 grant funding for attendees of DjangoCon Europe 2015; free tickets are also available.

The application process for financial assistance and free tickets to DjangoCon is now open.

If you need some financial assistance to attend, please apply now.

You can ask for a grant to cover accommodation and travel, for a free ticket, or both.

These grants and free tickets are for people like you. Don’t be shy about applying, or feel that you won’t be considered.

The US$5000 and tickets will be shared in the most useful way the DSF grants committee can find.

Please apply as soon as you can; applications close on 18th March. Announcements will be made as soon as the committee is able.

Anyone is welcome to apply for funding, but please be aware that there are always applications for more funds than are available, so sadly not everyone’s requests for funding can be met.

The DSF is able to provide these grants thanks to the continued support of donors who support its activities.
