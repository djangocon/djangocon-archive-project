Real Cardiff walking tour with Peter Finch

We have a wonderful treat in store for our DjangoCon attendees: a walking tour of Cardiff with Peter Finch. Places will be strictly limited, so don’t miss this opportunity - reserve yours now.

Peter Finch is a celebrated poet who’s also well-known for his series of Real Cardiff books, that seek out and uncover the city’s lost and forgotten secrets, especially some of the ones that have been deliberately forgotten.

He’ll take you on a walking tour in search of Cardiff (about 75 minutes), starting from our talks venue and making a wide circle through the centre that takes in some well-known landmarks with a hidden history, but also some unknown and mysterious ones, including:

  • the lost tunnel
  • fakes
  • a river that moved
  • secret passages
  • the psychic centre of Cardiff

You’ll get to see a few artworks and hear a few poems along the way, and by the end of it you’ll have a new insight into the city beneath your feet.

Tickets

The tours will take place on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday after the day’s talks to give as many people as possible an opportunity. A maximum of 30 people can be accommodated on each tour. There’s no charge, but you do need a ticket, so get yours as soon as you can. The walks will end in time for you to get to any dinners you have booked.
