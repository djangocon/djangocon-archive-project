Django Stars, our Gold sponsor

First-rate, gold-star supporters of the Django community, Django Stars are back for yet another DjangoCo, as our Gold sponsor.

Django Stars has been developing high-value web apps in Python/Django since 2008, and iOS/Android apps since 2013.

Today we are a top agile software development house in Europe with over 40 full-time employees (no freelancers), headquartered in Ukraine (Kiev) with offices in US (New Jersey) and Germany (Frankfurt am Main).

We focus on the development of complex solutions, that contribute to our clients’ success by becoming leaders in their markets. Django Stars works with startups, established businesses and the world’s leading brands from the US, Canada, UK, Norway, Switzerland, Germany, Ireland in the financial services, travel & hospitality, healthcare & fitness, real estate and transportation sectors.

Our clients

Among our clients are: Kaspersky Lab, NHST Media Group (Dn.no), MoneyPark, Diviac, Sindeo, Cmed Technology, MultiAd, VandaSecurities, Taunton Interactive, Cellosign, Lukkien, Unleash12, System Capital Management (SCM), Nabbesh, BDVA.

Our open-source commitment

We support and contribute to the Django ecosystem and participate at conferences such us: DjangoCon EU, PyCon UA, HotCode UA, IT JAM. We strongly believe that by co-operating in this Open Source community, we all grow together.
