JoinerySoft, our Silver sponsor

Joinerysoft is the leading supplier of software to the bespoke joinery market

Our mission to be the leading supplier of bespoke/custom joinery software means we never stand still and constantly push boundaries.

We are at the forefront conceptually designing new methodologies to combine 3D modelling, integrated ERP and costing all within web enabled software. We program in Django and C++.

Based in the Somerset town of Shepton Mallet our team of programmers have developed cutting edge software that leads the industry. Launched in 2004, our software is now used by over 700 customers throughout the world. We benefit from shared experience and knowledge from our sister company SomCom, who specialise in complex website and web application design and development.

If you are creative and like to push boundaries why not consider joining our programming team? Our team members work independently but within a team framework so you have to be able to work well with others. A love of maths, geometry and trigonometry would also help!

Joinerysoft’s IT Director will be speaking at DjangoCon so if you would like to chat about software development or job opportunities email us or send us a message on Twitter to arrange a time to meet up.
