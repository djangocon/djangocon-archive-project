Ticket prices for DjangoCon Europe 2015

We’ve spent days, and nights, agonising over ticket prices, crunching numbers, chewing pencils and examining our budget from every conceivable angle. Here, finally, are those prices.

We’ve made a huge effort to make the event as affordable as possible.

Your ticket includes:

  • all six days of the event - all the talks, tutorials, code sprints and clinics you desire
  • lunch and refreshments on all days
  • conference dinner
  • social events

Tickets sales will open on 1st January 2015; more details will be published then.


Early bird Standard
Individual £225 £275
Student £150 £200
Corporate £300 £350

Note: individual tickets are for those who are buying their own; corporate tickets for those whose employers are paying; student tickets are for those in full-time education. Employees of non-profit organisations may use the individual rate.
