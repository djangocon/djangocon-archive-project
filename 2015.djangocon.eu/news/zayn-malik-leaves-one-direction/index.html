Zayn Malik leaves One Direction

The DjangoCon Europe committee are shocked to learn that Zayn Malik has left One Direction

Citing stress, and the “desire to lead a normal life”, 22-year-old Zayn Malik has announced that he’s leaving the band he has been a part of for all of his adult life.

We’re sorry to learn that DjangoCon’s erstwhile boy-band nemesis (we hold them no ill-will for their lack of timing) is itself a victim of the pressures of fame and popularity. We know how tricky it is to organise a large event, but we hope it all goes well for them.

Zayn won’t be taking part in the band’s forthcoming European tour, so if he finds himself with a bit of spare time, he could do worse than to buy a ticket for DjangoCon, where he might find a whole new career to busy himself with.

Who knows, pop music’s loss might be web application development’s gain… but in the meantime, best wishes to Zayn while he relaxes and finds his new direction.

And seriously

Mental health issues are no joke at all. We’re taking them seriously, and doing a little about it. See Your wellbeing at DjangoCon Europe.
