Thorgate, our Silver sponsor

Web and mobile applications from Estonia with Python, Django & React

Founded in 2011, we are a team of dedicated designers, hardcore developers and business wizards. Our mission is to use the latest technologies to solve world’s problems. We believe that right people together can create amazing things.

In addition to software development to clients, we build our own startups. One of our most well-known startups is GateMe, winner of Seedcamp London 2013. Recently we launched Thorgate Ventures to distribute our experience further.

Two senior developers from Thorgate will be coming to DjangoCon. We’ll be happy to chat with you about software development, cooperation possibilities, HR and more. Visit our stand or tweet @thorgate to meet up!
