John Pinner

We are very sorry to hear of the death yesterday of John Pinner, a key member of the European Python community and a good friend to many in it.

John Pinner was well-known to many in the European and UK Python communities. He helped start the EuroPython and Python UK conferences, and for more than ten years was involved in organising them. He was the recipient of a PSF Community Award in 2010 for his work.

John was ever a friendly, welcoming presence. He was supportive and encouraging, generous with his time and pleased to help others in their own community efforts, and always modest about it.

Like many other people, we have good reason to remember John with fondness and gratitude.

Our thoughts are with his family and his close friends.

Thanks John, for your kindness and the wonderful contributions you made to our community. We’ll miss you.
