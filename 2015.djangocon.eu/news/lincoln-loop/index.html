Lincoln Loop, our Silver sponsor

Lincoln Loop is a full-service software development agency specializing in Python and Django development for web and mobile

Since 2007 Lincoln Loop’s emphasis on quality in an agile environment has helped numerous startups and high-traffic sites grow their businesses.

Lincoln Loop is a distributed team of developers and designers committed to building a better web.

In addition to building rock-solid Python-powered backends, Lincoln Loop also provides user experience, deployment, and real-time application development with JavaScript.

Current projects include:

  • BotBot.me, a beautiful web-based IRC logger
  • High Performance Django, the book on building and deploying fast, scalable Django sites
  • High Performance Django Expert Sessions, one-hour, in-depth consultations regarding developing, deploying and scaling complex Django applications
