Taiga, our Silver sponsor

Taiga is a project management platform for start-ups and agile developers and designers who want a simple, beautiful tool that helps make their work truly enjoyable.

Free. Open Source. Powerful.

Taiga is a project management platform for start-ups and agile developers & designers who want a simple, beautiful tool that makes work truly enjoyable.

Taiga is now the fastest growing Open Source Agile Project Management Tool featuring both Scrum and Kanban models. Since its public beta launch in September 30th it is home to 40.000+ projects all around the world and we estimate dozens of private instances are running behind many companies’ firewalls.

Taiga was created at Kaleidos, a Madrid-London company focused on technologically accelerating IT-heavy start-ups while nurturing employee entrepreneurship. Taiga is a good example of our experience helping building start-ups and good open source R&D.

Taiga is developed using a nice selection of open source technologies and components of which Django is likely to be the most important one.

Taiga (and our parent company, Kaleidos) are fully committed to Python, Django and Open Source in general and we will always try to help communities around these or similar concepts in any way we can.
