Divio, our Diamond sponsor

A DjangoCon is incomplete without Divio, one of the Django community’s most steadfast supporters

Divio has been producing fine web applications since 2001, and embraced Django in 2007.

The company has been proud to support the Django community and its projects over the years.

Divio’s django CMS, open-sourced in 2008, has grown into one of the most popular Django projects of all, and has been a gateway into Django and Python for hundreds of developers. django CMS powers this website too.

More recently Aldryn, Divio’s cloud platform for django CMS development and deployment, has lowered the barrier further and is bringing even more new users to Django.

DjangoCon Europe 2012 in Zürich was organised by Divio. Two of this year’s DjangoCon organisers, Stefanie and Daniele, work at Divio. The company is a major sponsor of Python and Django community conferences right across the calendar, from established events such as PyCon US to new ventures such as Python Namibia 2015 and initiatives including Django Girls.

As usual, Divio will be coming to DjangoCon with a number of their friendly staff. Say hello to them at the Divio booth, and stop for a chat about django CMS, Aldryn, working for Divio or anything else - they’ll be delighted to talk to you.
