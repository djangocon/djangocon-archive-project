Reckon Digital, our Gold sponsor

Technology, design and innovation. On demand.

Reckon Digital is a London-based technology consultancy.

We design and build custom software solutions, specialising in those contexts where AI is a technical advantage or where security is a primary concern.

Python, Django and the whole Free Software ecosystem play an important role in our work. For us, it’s achieving great results standing on the shoulders of giants.

Thanks to Django, we can build prototypes in a matter of hours and start a hands-on discussion with our clients from the very early stage. Later on during the development, Django helps stick to best-practices (eg: separation of concerns, tests, documentation) and achieve solid results.

We participate as much as possible in the Python and Django community and we love its friendly and open ethos.

Among the other Free and Open Source Software that we use on a daily basis, we are particularly fond of PostgreSQL, our database of choice. Robust, feature-rich and high-performant, PostgreSQL is a solid pillar in most of our projects.

Whenever we work with PostgreSQL and need some additional assistance, we rely on our friends at 2ndQuadrant, the world’s leading authority on PostgreSQL. We’re sharing a stand with them at DjangoCon, so come and say hello to us both there.

We are always happy to meet new people, drop us a line if you happen to be in town and have interest in our work. For contact and other details, visit our website.
