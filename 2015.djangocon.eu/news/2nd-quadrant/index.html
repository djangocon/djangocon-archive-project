2ndQuadrant, our Gold sponsor

Professional PostgreSQL for professional web developers

2ndQuadrant is an Oxford-based professional services company with offices in 6 regional offices around the world and staff in every continent.

We are major developers of PostgreSQL, with more than 10% of the core code written by 2ndQuadrant team members. We provide companies of all sizes with support, consultancy and training services for PostgreSQL

PostgreSQL is the world’s most advanced database, but that is more than just a tagline. No other database system has the heritage, robustness and performance of PostgreSQL. Combined with a roadmap of continuous innovation, PostgreSQL is the preferred choice of database for many Django developers.

2ndQuadrant is an independent, privately owned, debt-free company, funded by intellectual capital. We have no shareholders or investors to pay so any profits made pay for our developers to continuously improve PostgreSQL - improvements that users of open source PostgreSQL benefit from, as we continue to ensure the best database remains the best.

If you or your clients want the reassurance of a great database to support your web projects, choose PostgreSQL. If you or your clients want the reassurance of PostgreSQL expertise, choose 2ndQuadrant. In additional to code development, we provide training, consultancy and 24/7 technical support and will guarantee to fix a bug in PostgreSQL within 24 hours – a testimony to the skills of our team and our commitment to our clients.

Supporting open source software is at the heart of our business and of course our website is built on Django. To ensure our site is always running we partner with Reckon Digital, the London-based consultancy who have a fantastic reputation for their knowledge of Django. Reckon Digital are helping us to continuously transform our web user experience, and provide us with a great service we can rely on. We’re sharing a stand with them at DjangoCon, so come and say hello to us both there.

Find out more about us at 2ndQuadrant.
