PrimarySite, our Silver sponsor

PrimarySite build and host thousands of Django-based websites for primary schools around the UK.

PrimarySite is the leading and fastest growing provider of websites for primary schools in the UK.

They have produced more than 4,000 unique websites for primary schools, academies and nurseries in the UK and overseas. These are all powered by their easy-to-use CMS which is written in Python and makes extensive use of many Django components.

The Product Development team at PrimarySite is committed to building on its technical expertise with Django and related technologies, and very pleased to contribute towards furthering the technical expertise of others by sponsoring DjangoCon Europe.
