The DjangoCon Europe Crèche

There’ll be a free crèche at DjangoCon Europe for children up to 12 years old. Advance booking is required.

Our crèche will be run by Brecon Mobile Crèche. All the staff at the crèche are qualified child-care professionals, with a lot of experience running crèches at events of this kind.

The crèche will be suitably-equipped with toys, games and equipment for various activities, including outdoor activities if appropriate.

There will also be a bouncy castle (on which older DjangoCon attendees may be allowed to have a go, if they promise to behave nicely and come down when their turn is over).

Meals

If your child is old enough to have meals and refreshments with us, they are welcome to join us.

If they are not, you will need to provide their food.

Breastfeeding at DjangoCon

If you are breastfeeding your baby, we can provide a quiet room if that’s what you prefer. Otherwise, please feed your baby whenever and wherever you like, including during talks and at meal-times. DjangoCon will be a friendly environment for mothers and babies.
