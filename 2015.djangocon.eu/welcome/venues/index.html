Venues

The three venues that will serve us over the six days of DjangoCon are lovely examples of early 20th-century architecture. They’re all located very close to each other in the city’s Cathays Park, considered one the finest civic centres in Britain.

All the venues are a very short walk from the city centre and most of its hotels, and about 15-20 minutes from Cardiff Central railway station. See below for addresses and maps.
