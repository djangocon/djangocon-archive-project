Thursday evening party

To conclude a day of hard work at the code sprints, workshops and clinics, a party with food and drink

After the day’s work, a party with food and drinks at Cardiff's beloved Chapter Arts Centre.

There’ll be a barbecue, and a wide selection of vegetarian and vegan food.

The food and drinks are included in your DjangoCon ticket, but you must tell us if you’re coming, so we know how many people to expect.

If you don’t tell us in advance, you won’t get a ticket to the party. This is part of our policy on food waste.

To tell us you’ll be coming to the party:

  • find the email message with your DjangoCon ticket from Ti.to
  • use the link to open the ticket on the Ti.to website,
  • select Change or Complete ticket
  • make sure that you have indicated you’re coming to the party (Party evening Thursday 4th June on the ticket)

Chapter Arts Centre is about 30 minutes' walk across Bute Park and over the river from our venue (or about 25 minutes’ via a less scenic route).
