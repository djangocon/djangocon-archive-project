Advanced django CMS

A hands-on workshop for developers who want to dive deeper into django CMS and its rich ecosystem.

Note: this session will run alongside the conference sprints. It’s free to all conference attendees.

About the workshop

This hands-on in-depth workshop with core developers of django CMS will take you through some of its advanced features and lead you through topics such as the creation of plugins and integration with other applications.

Bring your django CMS projects, your difficult questions and your ideas!

About Stefan Foulis

Stefan Foulis is a Python developer with many years of Django experience. He works at Divio, which has backed django CMS since its infancy, helping it become an open-source success story.

Stefan is a core developer of django CMS; he has made substantial contributions to the project and worked on countless django CMS websites. He’s also the lead developer of Aldryn, a cloud-based hosting/development platform for django CMS.
