The code doctor is in

Do you have a Python/Django problem? Take it to a code clinic, where an experienced Django programmer will help you find a solution.

Note: the clinic will run alongside the conference sprints. It’s free to all conference attendees.

Some of the world’s most experienced Django programmers, including many members of the Django project's own core team, have offered to put themselves at your disposal, to help you with your Python/Django problems.

Think of it as a free personal consultation with a wise and kindly code doctor.

Whatever the nature of your Django problem, you’ll get the undivided attention of a bona-fide Django expert. They may or may not be able to solve it for you on the spot, but they will give you their time to look at it with you, and at the end of it you should have a clear idea of where the solution lies.

Don’t hesitate to take advantage of their generosity! They’ll be only too pleased to help you. One of them commented to us: “Fixing other people’s bugs is fun! Much more fun than fixing your own…”

We are very grateful of course to all those who have offered their services for this code clinic. If you too would like to help staff the code clinic, just get in touch.
