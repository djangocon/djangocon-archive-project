Jog, Shower, Code: 5km fun run

We'll go for a run through some of Cardiff's most beautiful green spaces and along the river.

Eager to get some exercise? Want to see a beautiful park? Don’t enjoy being asleep in the morning?

Then this is for you!

We’ll meet outside Cardiff City Hall and go for a run - through Bute Park and Pontcanna Fields, over the Blackweir Bridge and back to Bute Park by the woods along the river. You’ll probably see swans, egrets and cormorants; it’s beautiful.

The planned route is just over 5km and is very flat; if you want less exercise rather than more, it’s easy to cut it short.

All levels of fitness are welcome. You’ll be accompanied by Vince (and perhaps Auraya the puppy).

Date & time
2nd June 2015, 07.00
