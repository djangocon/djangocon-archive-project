Code

Two days of Django sprints, workshops and clinics. Turn up, join in and be part of the most rewarding two days of the whole conference. All sessions take place at Cardiff University.

Lunch and refreshments will be provided.

To make the experience even more memorable, there’ll be a party with food and drinks on Thursday evening at Chapter Arts Centre.

Sprints

Code sprints provide members of the global community to collaborate in person to tackle outstanding issues or developments in a project’s code-base. They are huge fun.

Let us know if you’d like to lead a sprint for a particular project so we can advertise it.

We want as many people as possible to stay for the sprints - particularly people who haven’t taken part before.

To encourage people to join in, we’ll be supporting the sprints with a “Don't be afraid to commit” workshop, aimed specially at those who’d like to take part but aren’t sure they have the skills - yet.

Here’s everything you wanted to know about code sprints but were afraid to ask.
