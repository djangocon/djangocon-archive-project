DjangoCon Europe 2015 in Cardiff, Wales

Sunday

Open day

31st May - a day of free introductory talks and tutorials, to showcase Django to a wider audience and help newcomers get the most from DjangoCon

Monday-Wednesday

Talks

1st-3rd June - three days of talks on Django-related subjects of all kinds: a single track of longer talks, shorter talks and lightning talks

Thursday/Friday

Sprints, tutorials, clinics

4th-5th June - two days of code sprints, workshops and clinics: contribute, learn new skills and get help with your Django problems from an expert
