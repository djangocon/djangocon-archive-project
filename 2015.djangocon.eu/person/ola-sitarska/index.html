Ola Sitarska

Committee member

About

Ola was one of the organisers (and Chief Swag Manager) of Django: Under the Hood in Amsterdam 2014. Together with her amazing team, she also organised DjangoCon Europe 2013 in Warsaw (the circus one!).

In July 2014, she co-founded Django Girls, a non-profit organisation that helps women learn how to build websites using Django.

Ola is a board member of the Django Software Foundation and works for Potato in London.

At DjangoCon Europe, you’ll probably find her hanging decorations and laughing at Batistek's jokes.
