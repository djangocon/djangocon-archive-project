Daniele Procida

Chair

About

Daniele was the organiser of Django Weekend Cardiff, and a member of the organising committee of DjangoCon Europe 2014. He’s also leading the organisation of Python Namibia 2015, the first-ever Python conference to be held in the country.

He is a member of the Django Project core team, and works for Divio. At Divio he works on django CMS, Aldryn and other fun Django-based projects.

At the DjangoCon Europe sprints he’ll be running a Don’t be afraid to commit workshop, for people who would like to contribute to open source projects but aren’t sure how to get started.
