About

Vince is a mathematician at Cardiff University, and teaches Python to undergraduate maths students.

He helped with the running of Django Weekend Cardiff and will be participating at Python Namibia 2015, the first-ever Python conference to be held in the country.

Vince is an active contributor to Sage and loves all things open source, using Python and Django in his research and teaching.
